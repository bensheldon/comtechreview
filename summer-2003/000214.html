Pre-Conference Sessions
by anonymous

Thanks to support from the U.S. Department of Health and Human Services Compassion Capital Fund, CTCNet is pleased to present a range of Pre-Conference workshops offering more in-depth exploration of issues and resources useful to our members' work.

2003 sessions include:

Access Aware: Making Your CTC Accessible to People With Disabilities
Curriculum Development for Youth Technology Programs--The YouthLearn Method
Creating a Realistic Fundraising Plan
ScienceQuest: Successful After-School Learning
The CTC VISTA Project: Orientation, Update, and Training Sessions

Fuller information on these sessions can be found on CTCNet's 2003 Conference Website.
