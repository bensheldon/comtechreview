Community Networking Basics — Some Key Sites

The Association for Community Networking (AFCN), www.afcn.org — note discussion lists and forums, membership services and information including directory of members.

Frank Odasz/lone-eagles.com: Frank's articles in CTR archives (see sidebar), lone-eagle VISTA Resources and blog, compilation of Best Community Networking Resources and Articles, Community Wireless and Satellite Internet Resources, and Resource Listings.

Civic and Community Networking syllabus, Randy Groves and Doug Schuler, Evergreen State College, Olympia, Washington

Training Materials: Learn About Wireless Networking, the Association for Progressive Communications (APC)

Community Connections: Preserving Local Values in the Information Age, Technology Opportunities Program, Department of Commerce, National Telecommunications and Information Administration, (NTIA), September 2000, by Christopher Conte with Judith Sparrow and Don Druker.

New Aboriginal Youth Content Management System at deadlyzone.org. Web-authoring and collaboration tools from the deadlymob.org CTC, an indigenous online community and in Alice Springs, NT (Northern Territory), Australia.

Donnie Morrison's Scottish Telework Projecthas pooled the potential online employee resources of the Outer Hebrides of Scotland to become a leader in e-working by acting as an outsourcing location for customer contact centers.

Tripod's Free Ecommerce websites and tools.
