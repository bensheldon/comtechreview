CTC VISTA Project Update
by Peter Miller

This fall the CTC VISTA Project began its third year, continuing to draw the most inquiries and applications of all AmeriCorps programs in its online information and recruitment system.  In August, the project PreService Orientation program welcomed its largest contingency yet.

 

Pictured here, with site placements and additional information available in the project directory, are:

Back row, l to r: Paul Savage, Amy McKenzie, Matthew Frank, Michelle Walson, Daniel Sheen, Margaret Goodwyn, James Fishwick, David Siegal, Kenneth Yu, Harold McNaron, VISTA Leader Matt Crichton, Melissa Daigle.

Middle row, l to r: Sharon Alhourani, Kristine "Krissy" Sinor, Colette Mahoney, Chaneqa Wooten, Heidi Marshall, Beth Joseph, Susan Wong, Edith Yang.

Kneeling:  Adam Cohen, Scott Hillier, project staff Liz Barnes and Peter Miller, Shubhada Kambli, April Carvalho.

  

Not Pictured: Charlotte Crockford and Karen “Cookie” Sheers, and Jay Oliver.
