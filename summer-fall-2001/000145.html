The Alliance for Community Media
by anonymous

The Community Media Review is the quarterly journal of the Alliance for Community Media. Published for nearly 25 years in print, CMR On Line highlights selected articles, web-links and other information resources in the most current issue. It contains a section on advertising and editorial policy and will soon contain an expanded version of the print issue, with more commentary from its readers and archives of past issues and articles tracing the evolution of community television.

Jeff Hansell is Executive Director of Malden Access TV and CMR Online webmaster.
