Recent TA to Nonprofit Resources of Note
by Steven Clift and Edward Batista

"More than Bit Players: How Information Technology Will Change the Ways Nonprofits and Foundations Work and Thrive in the Information Age," a Surdna report by Andrew Blau. "Andrew Blau is one of most respected leaders in the non-profit and foundation world when it comes to information age trends and strategies." -- Steven Clift, Democracies Online

"Michael Stein interview with Bill Pease," . Michael Stein is an Internet strategist and consultant with 15 years experience working with nonprofits; Bill Pease was one of the leaders driving Environmental Defense's early Internet efforts, and is now Vice President of Product Delivery at GetActive Software. GetActive, formerly known as LocusPocus, is an Application Service Provider (ASP) that uses technology developed at Environmental Defense to help nonprofits communicate more effectively with their members and supporters. "I found this to be an exceptionally frank discussion of a number of obstacles that Bill and his peers encountered and strategies that they deployed as they helped to make Environmental Defense one of the most Internet-savvy nonprofits in the U.S." -- Edward Batista, Executive Director, the Nonprofit Technology Enterprise Network (NTEN)
